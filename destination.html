New Zealand

Tour Highlights:

Auckland:

Begin your adventure in the bustling city of Auckland, known as the "City of Sails." Explore its iconic landmarks, vibrant waterfront, and diverse culinary scene.

Rotorua:

Delve into the heart of Maori culture and geothermal wonders. Visit Te Puia to witness the Pohutu Geyser and experience traditional Maori performances.

Waitomo Caves:

Embark on a magical boat ride through the glowworm-lit caves, a natural phenomenon unique to New Zealand.

Queenstown:

Known as the adventure capital, Queenstown offers adrenaline-pumping activities like bungee jumping, skydiving, and jet boating.

Milford Sound: Cruise through this stunning fjord, surrounded by towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls.

Lake Tekapo:

Relax by the turquoise waters of Lake Tekapo and stargaze at the Aoraki Mackenzie International Dark Sky Reserve.
